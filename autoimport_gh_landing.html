How it works

From preference to
parking spot in 4 steps

Your dedicated agent handles everything — from finding your car to delivering it to your door.

1
Tell us what you want

Open the app, set your make, model, source country, condition and budget. Takes less than 2 minutes.

2
Meet your agent

A dedicated import agent is assigned within 2 hours. They search auctions and dealers on your behalf.

3
Approve and pay

Your agent sends vehicle options in chat with full cost breakdowns. You approve, they handle the rest.

4
Car delivered to you

Shipping, port clearance, and delivery — all tracked in the app in real time. No surprises.
